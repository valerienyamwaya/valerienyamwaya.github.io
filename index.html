• About me

I am curious about the stories that live in culture—how they are carried through fashion, music, art, memory, and everyday life. My work brings together creative entrepreneurship, cultural research, and community engagement to explore how heritage shapes identity and inspires new possibilities. At the heart of everything I do is a belief that culture is not only something we inherit, but something we actively shape, share, and carry into the future.
